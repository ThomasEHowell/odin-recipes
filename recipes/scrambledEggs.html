Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a classic! These delicious high protein, nutritious eggs can be the cornerstone of your healthy diet. This simple dish can be made be almost anyone with just a tad of practice. Once you can cook scrambled eggs, you can even think of using them as a piece of other more interesting dishes, but that's a story for another day....

Ingredients

  • As many eggs as desired (two to six recommended)
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Spices: salt, black pepper

Steps

  1. Place pan on stove and turn stove onto high.
  2. Crack eggs on side of bowl and drop their contents into bowl.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and a likewise small amount of milk.
  4. With nearly any piece of silverware, stir the bowl until its contents fall together.
  5. Add one tablespoon of butter to the pan. Spread it around with a spatula until it dissolves fully.
  6. Pour the eggs from the bowl into the pan.
  7. As the eggs solidify, use the spatula to break them up and to flip them around to even out the heating.
  8. Just before the eggs are fully cooked, turn off the stove. The heat will continue cooking them.
  9. Pour the now scrambled eggs back into the bowl. You are now finished.
