Protein Shake

Getting your protein in might be tough for you, but it doesn't need to be! Introducing our protein shake. A nice healthy shake that you can make in just five to ten minutes which will satisfy much of your daily protein needs without needing too many calories. What's more is we didn't use any amount of artificial whey protein powder in our recipe so as to save ourselves from their terrible energy crashes. We hope you enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Any blender that can accommodate four cups of liquids and solids
  • A food scale
  • Two cups of Fairlife's fat free milk
  • Twenty grams of old fashioned oats
  • Fourteen grams of stevia
  • Four grams of creatine
  • Two and a half servings of peanut butter (80 grams)
  • One medium-sized banana

Steps

  1. Add two cups of Fairlife.
  2. Using a spoon, add fourteen grams of stevia and four grams of creatine.
  3. Now use the spoon to add 80 grams of peanut butter.
  4. Add the banana.
  5. Blend for 30-60 seconds.
  6. Pour shake into drinking container.
  7. Hand wash the blender sooner rather than later or else the grime will take longer to clean off. Finished.
